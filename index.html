what is this site about

I was only nine years old. I loved ducks so much, I had photos and posters of them. I'd pray to ducks every night before I go to bed, thanking for the life I've been given. "Ducks are love", I would say, "Ducks are life". My dad hears me and calls me a schizo stoner. I knew he was just jealous for my devotion of the waddle. I call him a goose-lover. He slaps me and sends me to go to sleep. I'm crying now and my face hurts. I lay in bed and it's really cold. A warmth is moving towards me. I feel something touch me. These are ducks. I'm so happy. They quack smoothly in my ear, "This is my pond". They grab me with their powerful feet, and take me through the window. We start to fly. The view is majestic. As we are flying back home, they suddenly throw me violently through the window. It hurts so much, but I know what I must do. I can feel my body sliced by the broken shards, but the power of Duck empoweres me. I sprint to the kitchen. I want to please The Great Duck Swarm. They let out a mighty stoner quack, as I throw them some edibles. My dad walks in. Ducks look him straight in the eyes, and quack out, "We're now ducked up". Ducks fly through the broken window with him. I knew it's the last time I will see my dad, yet I don't cry. Ducks are love. Ducks are life.
